Accounting Software Academy

ASA(Accounting Software Academy) is the fully hands on program we initiated to bridge the gap between classroom learning and labor market in the area of Accounting, Management and Finance to help students find their dream jobs or create their own with confidence and competence with “QuickBooks”. This guarantees the success and marketability of your Students and your institution

PROBLEM WORTH SOLVING

Rwanda and the world in general is quickly adapting technology in all sectors of life. However, looking at the academic Curriculum, it does not move with the acceleration of update in variants and versions in the technology field. This shortcoming created a very big gap comparing acquired skills from classroom to the labor market requirements where graduates find themselves lost!

Vision

Increasing digital career readiness by developing 21st century skills through personal finance and entrepreneurship education.

Mission

  • Creating new opportunities
  • Revitalizing communities
  • Expanding possibilities.

COMMUNITY IMPACT

In 10 years of operation, KURANGA DIGITAL Ltd together with our partners, have had a significant impact on the community and the country economic ,through which the major focus is to improve both Hands on skills Education , businesses & NGOs operations in the competitive digital finance tool “QuickBooks” through capacity building program (ASA) and consultancy . We improved the labor market fit skills for students, graduates and job seekers where more than 75% from “ASA” were linked with jobs. Our program increases students confidence ,helps their resumes stand out ,increases the value of Academics and provides also additional recruitment tool to Academics program.

Beneficiaries

ASA program intends to help both Schools and students to enhance practical learning skills in the field of accounting and finance and also to access in full all QuickBooks toolkits.


My Pathprener

MyPathpreneur program is specifically designed for passionate entrepreneurs aiming to strengthen their potential in managing business by equipping them with practical financial literacy and introducing innovative technology tools in finance (QuickBooks).

PROBLEM WORTH SOLVING

Most businesses are created by passionate entrepreneurs who significantly lack financial and business management skills in their operations. Thus businesses are unable to survive on the market on the long run even if those businesses are making good sales on a regular basis, simply because such entrepreneurs have insufficient modern skills to manage company’s resources

Vision

Increasing digital career readiness by developing 21st century skills through personal finance and entrepreneurship education.

Mission

  • Creating new opportunities
  • Revitalizing communities
  • Expanding possibilities through enhanced technology based skills.

IMPACT TO ENTREPRENEURSHIP

In 10 years of operation, KURANGA DGITAL Ltd has had a significant impact on the community through which the major focus is improve business operations & services using digital finance tools such as QuickBooks. Kuranga Digital embarked on its role in bridging the gap in the labor market and education scheme then opened a training facility which improved labor market fit-tech skills in finance for students ,graduates to embark and grow existing startups. Partnering with you can be of a useful impact to both of us, your beneficiaries and the country. Adults learn better by participation practices with evidence, QuickBooks is all about that in the area of finance and accounting.

Beneficiaries

MyPathpreneur intends to empower young entrepreneurs, women-led businesses and SME’s in hands-on financial skills by using QuickBooks.
