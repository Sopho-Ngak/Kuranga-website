Accounting Software Academy

Accounting Software Academy is the fully hands on program we initiated to bridge the gap between classroom learning and labor market in the area of Accounting, Management and Finance to help students find their dream jobs or create their own with confidence and competence with “QuickBooks”. This guarantees the success and marketability of your Students and your institution

Rwanda and the world in general is quickly adapting technology in all sectors of life. However, looking at the academic Curriculum, it does not move with the acceleration of update in variants and versions in the technology field. This shortcoming created a very big gap comparing acquired skills from classroom to the labor market requirements where graduates find themselves lost!

Our vision is to Increase digital career readiness by developing 21st century skills through personal finance and entrepreneurship education.

ASA program intends to help both Schools and students to enhance practical learning skills in the field of accounting and finance and also to access in full all QuickBooks toolkits.

My Pathprener

Most businesses are created by passionate entrepreneurs who significantly lack financial and business management skills in their operations. MyPathpreneur program is specifically designed for passionate entrepreneurs aiming to strengthen their potential in managing business by equipping them with practical financial literacy and introducing innovative technology tools in finance

Thus businesses are unable to survive on the market on the long run even if those businesses are making good sales on a regular basis, simply because such entrepreneurs have insufficient modern skills to manage company’s resources

Kuranga Digital embarked on its role in bridging the gap in the labor market and education scheme then opened a training facility which improved labor market fit-tech skills in finance for students ,graduates to embark and grow existing startups.

MyPathpreneur intends to empower young entrepreneurs, women-led businesses and SME’s in hands-on financial skills by using QuickBooks.
