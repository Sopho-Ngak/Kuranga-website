Get to know about our company

KURANGA DIGITAL LTD is expecting to be a major player in the region. With its expertise of six years in office automation and consultancy services as well as its strong presence, the Company can anticipate its customers’ needs, from corporate, small and medium enterprises to large enterprise businesses but not only that, the company is continually contributing to the social economic development especially in information technology orientation. The company is reaching that with support of KIGALI CITY in the program of fighting against unemployment and poverty. KURANGA DIGITAL LTD empowers mainly youth in job creation and job searching with competitive skills by providing trainings in Software development and in accounting technology tool “QuickBooks” which is a dominating accounting software on the labor market now days.
